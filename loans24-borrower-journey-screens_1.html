The two moats

Speed is already built — and Spinny has it too — so it isn't the edge. Reading risk precisely is, and it pays off twice:

Moat 1 · Out-approve
Approve the borrowers Spinny rejects.

Moat 2 · Out-price
Move the rate toward the bank's.

▼ Lower interest

The Spinny model — fast, but it can't follow

Spinny's journey is sleek; the limit isn't speed. It's that the credit decision isn't theirs, and it's tuned for prime. Here's where Rajesh falls out — and exactly where our two moats win.

1 · One-click financing
Frictionless, inside a premium buying flow. Only Spinny-assured cars, so valuation is pre-automated. We match this — speed isn't the gap.
2 · Credit routed to partners
The decision is outsourced to ~18 finance partners and approved at their discretion. Spinny doesn't own the risk engine.
